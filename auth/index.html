Terms & Privacy
Please read and accept before using VAULT.
How it works
VAULT generates one-time passwords (TOTP) entirely on your device. Your secret keys and credentials are stored only in your browser's local storage. Nothing is ever transmitted to any server — we have no backend, no accounts, no cloud sync.
Your responsibility
You are solely responsible for safeguarding your secret keys. If you clear your browser data or uninstall the app, your 2FA secrets will be permanently lost unless you have backed them up. Always keep your original backup codes from each service.
No warranties
VAULT is provided "as is" without any warranty. We are not liable for account lockouts, data loss, or security incidents resulting from use of this app. Use at your own risk and always maintain backup recovery methods for every account you add.
Privacy
We collect zero personal data. No analytics, no tracking, no cookies beyond local storage. Your secrets never leave your device. This app works fully offline after first load.
Acceptable use
You agree to use VAULT only for lawful purposes and only with 2FA secrets you are authorised to use. Do not use this app to access accounts that do not belong to you.
