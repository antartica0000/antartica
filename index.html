About Antartica.Org
Antartica.Org
Explore the frozen continent

Antartica.Org is an informational hub dedicated to the world's southernmost continent — its science, ecology, geopolitics, and centuries of exploration history.

Mission

We believe knowledge about Antarctica matters — for climate science, biodiversity, and the future of our planet. This site gathers the best resources in one place, freely available to everyone.
